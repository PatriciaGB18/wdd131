Welcome to Flavors & More

This is the planning document for the Flavors & More website, which focuses on delicious recipes for desserts and Sunday lunches. The final website will provide a collection of carefully curated recipes for those looking for inspiration in the kitchen.

Site Purpose

Flavors & More is a website dedicated to providing delicious and easy-to-follow recipes, with a special focus on desserts and Sunday lunch ideas. Whether you're looking for a sweet treat or a comforting meal to share with family, you'll find inspiration here.

Scenarios

  • What are some easy and delicious dessert recipes I can make at home?
  • What is a great recipe for a traditional Sunday lunch with family?

Color Scheme & Typography

Primary Color: Warm Orange (used for accents and buttons)

Secondary Colors: Complementary neutral tones for background and text.

Typography:

  • Playfair Display: Used for headings.
  • Lato: Used for body text.
  • Dancing Script: Used for special text elements.
